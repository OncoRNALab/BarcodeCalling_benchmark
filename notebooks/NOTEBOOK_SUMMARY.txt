================================================================================
KEY FINDINGS (from notebook)
================================================================================

TOOL PERFORMANCE:
- All tools perform well at low error rates
- Performance degradation varies by tool at high error rates
- Precision generally more stable than recall

ERROR RATE EFFECTS:
- Low: Minimal performance impact
- Medium: Moderate challenges, tool differences emerge
- High: Significant challenges, trade-offs required

BARCODE COUNT EFFECTS:
- 21K: Best performance (less complexity)
- 42K: Moderate performance
- 85K: Most challenging (increased ambiguity)

TOOL CHARACTERISTICS:
- RandomBarcodes: High precision, GPU-accelerated
- QUIK: Balanced performance, GPU-accelerated
- Columba: CPU-based, good alternative
